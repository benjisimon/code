DSLR

Basic Settings

  • Lens: Wide Angle
  • Focus: Manual
  • White Balance: Auto
  • Aperture: f/4
  • ISO: 1600
  • Exposure length: 25 seconds

Tips

  • Avoid nights with a full moon
  • Use the countdown timer to avoid camera shake
  • To focus on a star, zoom in (including digital zoom) and manually focus the camera
  • Star trailing will begin after 15 to 20 seconds
  • Wider aperture is better
  • ISO 800 is also an option. Too high and there's too much noise, too low and you won't capture enough light.
